Helpers

Helpers are custom mixins and functions that help ease the development of your application.

See the Pen Helper Example by Jason Bellamy (@jasonbellamy) on CodePen.

How do I document them?

Helpers should be documented with a name, description, and category.

How do I structure their files & folders?

helpers
├── functions
│   └── _functions.scss
└── mixins
    ├── _animations.scss
    ├── _gradients.scss
    └── _transforms.scss

Generator

You can automatically create and @import helper files with the Plum Generator by running the following commands:

  • yo plum:function <name>
  • yo plum:mixin <name>
